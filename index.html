About Me

I am a physics graduate and researcher in Quantum Information Science, with contributions to state discrimination methods and quantum teleportation protocols. Skilled in analytical problem-solving, teaching, and quantum circuit simulation using Qiskit and PennyLane. I am passionate about advancing quantum communication and computation.
